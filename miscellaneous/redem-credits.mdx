## What are ReDem Credits?

ReDem Credits are the currency used to pay for quality checks on your survey data.

You can purchase credits through annual subscription plans. To learn more about available plans or to sign up [click here](https://redem.io/pricing/).

## Basics of ReDem Credits

Analyzing data with ReDem requires ReDem credits, with the amount depending on the quality checks selected for each respondent.

The minimum cost is 4 credits per respondent, with additional credits required based on the quality parameters you choose. This flexible approach allows you to balance costs while achieving your desired level of data quality!

### ðŸ’¡Important Credit Rules

* **Grid-Question Score (GQS):** The maximum charge is capped at **2 credits per respondent**. Each grid-question item **costs 0.05 credits**, but even if a respondent has numerous items, the total cost for GQS will not exceed 2 credits.

* **Time Score (TS):** The maximum cost is **1 credit per respondent**, regardless of the number of time-related data points included.

* **Coherence Score (CHS):** A maximum of **100 data questions** are included in the base calculation, costing **4 credits** per respondent. For additional question beyond 100, each incurs an extra cost of **0.03 credits**.

* **No Credits for Empty Answers or Missing Values:** Credits are only applied to valid, present data points. No credits are charged for empty responses or missing values.

* **Total Credit Calculation for Surveys**: The total cost of a survey is calculated by adding up the credit costs for all respondents. Each respondent's credit usage is determined by their individual data points and the quality checks applied.
