The ReDem is designed to enhance your survey data quality with **AI-driven quality assurance**. From evaluating and calculating ReDem Scores to filtering out low-quality respondents, ReDem provides all the tools you need to make your data more reliable and valuable.

The ReDem documentation enables you to gain a deeper understanding of what ReDem is, and how best to use it for your organization’s use case.

## New to ReDem?
If you're new to ReDem, a great place to start is by [setting up your account](/account-setup). Follow these steps to get everything ready for your journey with ReDem!

## Learn how ReDem works
To get a better understanding of how ReDem works and what it can do, you can read more about [how it works](/how-it-works).
